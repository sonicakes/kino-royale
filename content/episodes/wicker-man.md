Who would the Film Lady put inside her Wicker Man? **8/10 Rowan Hares** for 1973 folk horror classic - The Wicker Man.

In the fourth episode of Kino Royale, The Film Lady goes deeper — into the ancient roots of paganism, the Slavic gods, the Russian double faith, and why the old religion never actually died. From Beltane bonfires to birthday candles, from Dazbog to the Wicker Man's final flame — plus a genuine Russian separation spell to close, because some episodes demand it.
